# Privacy and GDPR Policy

## Privacy-by-Design

Northstar applies privacy-by-design principles during discovery, solution design, implementation, and support. Personal data requirements are identified early, including lawful basis, data categories, data subjects, processing purposes, retention periods, access roles, and cross-border transfer considerations.

## Data Minimization

Solutions should collect only the personal data required for the agreed business purpose. Optional fields should be clearly identified. Sensitive information should not be placed in logs, analytics events, email notifications, or support tickets unless there is a documented need and appropriate controls.

## Data Subject Rights

Where required, systems can support data subject request workflows such as access, correction, deletion, restriction, and export. The implementation approach depends on the customer role, data controller responsibilities, retention obligations, and integrations with source systems.

## Retention and Deletion

Retention periods are configured based on customer policy and legal requirements. Northstar recommends documented retention schedules and automated deletion or anonymization where feasible. Backup retention may differ from application-level deletion timelines and should be disclosed during solution design.

## Consent and Notices

When consent or privacy notices are required, the platform can display notice text, record acceptance events, and store versioned notice metadata. Customers remain responsible for approving legal wording unless Northstar is explicitly contracted for legal content support.

## Data Processing Agreement

Northstar can work under a data processing agreement defining processing purpose, confidentiality, security measures, subprocessors, breach notification, assistance with data subject rights, deletion/return of data, and audit rights. Final legal terms require human legal review.

## International Transfers

For EU personal data, hosting region and subprocessors should be reviewed. Northstar can design deployments to keep primary data storage in an agreed region, subject to selected cloud services and third-party integrations.
