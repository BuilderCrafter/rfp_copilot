# Case Study: Healthcare Appointment Preparation Portal

## Client Context

A healthcare services organization wanted a patient-facing portal for appointment preparation, document collection, pre-visit forms, and secure staff review. The organization needed privacy controls, accessible design, and integration with scheduling data.

## Scope

Northstar delivered patient registration, secure document upload, configurable pre-visit questionnaires, staff review queues, status notifications, audit logging, and integration with the customer's scheduling export. The portal was designed for mobile and desktop use.

## Privacy and Security

The solution minimized sensitive data in notifications, restricted staff access by role, encrypted data in transit and at rest, and captured audit logs for support and administrative activity. Legal privacy notices were supplied and approved by the customer.

## User Experience

The project included prototype review, plain-language content revisions, responsive design testing, keyboard navigation checks, and usability sessions with staff representatives.

## Outcomes

The portal reduced incomplete pre-visit paperwork, improved staff visibility of missing documents, and created a foundation for future integrations with electronic health record systems. HL7/FHIR integration was identified as a future phase requiring specialist assessment.
