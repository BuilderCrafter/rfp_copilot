# Previous RFP Answer: Implementation Plan and Governance

## Question

Provide your implementation methodology and governance model.

## Answer

We propose an iterative delivery methodology with phases for mobilization, discovery, solution design, build and configuration, integration, testing, user acceptance, go-live, and hypercare. During mobilization, we confirm roles, meeting cadence, repositories, communication channels, risk log, decision log, project plan, and initial assumptions.

Discovery workshops identify business processes, user groups, compliance needs, integrations, reporting needs, data flows, and acceptance criteria. Outputs include a prioritized backlog, process maps, integration inventory, and open-question register. Delivery then proceeds in sprints with backlog refinement, implementation, peer review, testing, sprint review, and planning.

Governance normally includes weekly delivery status meetings, sprint reviews, risk and issue tracking, decision logs, and steering meetings for senior stakeholders. User acceptance testing is supported through agreed UAT scenarios mapped to business processes. Training and handover include administrator training, support runbooks, release notes, and knowledge-transfer sessions.
