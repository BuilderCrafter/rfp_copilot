# RFP: Smart City Citizen Services Portal

## 1. Background

The City of Northbridge is seeking a vendor to design, implement, and support a citizen services portal. The portal will allow residents to submit service requests, check permit status, receive notifications, and access municipal information from desktop and mobile devices.

The city expects the vendor to reuse proven delivery methods, provide a secure cloud-hosted service, integrate with existing municipal systems, and support the portal after launch.

## 2. Vendor Experience

2.1 Describe your experience delivering digital services platforms for public-sector or regulated organizations.

2.2 Provide at least two relevant case studies showing similar implementation scope, integrations, user adoption, and measurable outcomes.

2.3 Explain how your team handles projects where business users, IT teams, legal stakeholders, and external vendors all need to contribute requirements.

## 3. Functional Requirements

3.1 Describe how your platform supports account registration, service request submission, status tracking, and resident notifications.

3.2 Explain how your solution can support multilingual content and future service categories without major code changes.

3.3 Provide your approach to role-based administration for city staff who need to triage, assign, and close resident requests.

## 4. Integrations and Data

4.1 Describe your approach to integrating with existing CRM, GIS, document management, and payment systems.

4.2 Explain how your API design supports secure data exchange, error handling, rate limiting, and auditability.

4.3 Describe your data migration approach for importing existing service request history and resident contact records.

## 5. Security, Privacy, and Compliance

5.1 Describe your information security controls, including encryption, identity and access management, audit logging, and vulnerability management.

5.2 Explain your approach to privacy-by-design and handling personal data under GDPR or equivalent privacy regulations.

5.3 Describe whether your organization is ISO 27001 certified, SOC 2 audited, or aligned to comparable controls.

5.4 Explain how security incidents are detected, escalated, communicated, and remediated.

## 6. Accessibility and User Experience

6.1 Describe your accessibility approach and whether you support WCAG 2.2 AA requirements.

6.2 Explain how you validate usability for citizens with different technical skill levels and accessibility needs.

## 7. Implementation and Governance

7.1 Provide your proposed implementation methodology, including discovery, design, configuration, integration, testing, deployment, and handover.

7.2 Describe your project governance model, including steering meetings, sprint ceremonies, risk tracking, and decision logs.

7.3 Explain how you will support user acceptance testing and training for city staff.

## 8. Hosting, Operations, and Support

8.1 Describe the hosting architecture, monitoring approach, backup strategy, and disaster recovery capabilities.

8.2 Provide your standard support model, support hours, incident severity levels, and response targets.

8.3 Explain how you manage releases, maintenance windows, rollback plans, and service communications.

## 9. Commercial Response

9.1 Provide your pricing model, including implementation, licensing, hosting, support, and optional future enhancements.

9.2 Describe assumptions, exclusions, and customer responsibilities that affect pricing or delivery timeline.

## 10. Requested Attachments

10.1 Provide sample project plan.

10.2 Provide resumes for proposed key personnel.

10.3 Provide data processing agreement and cyber insurance certificate.
