# RFP: Digital KYC and AML Onboarding Platform

## 1. Procurement Context

A fintech institution is procuring a digital onboarding platform for retail and small-business customers. The solution must support identity verification, customer due diligence, risk scoring, case management, audit evidence, and integration with core banking and fraud-monitoring systems.

## 2. Vendor and Delivery Experience

2.1 Describe your experience with financial-services or other regulated onboarding platforms.

2.2 Provide examples of projects involving identity verification, case management, compliance workflows, or integration with core business systems.

2.3 Explain how you balance rapid delivery with regulatory review and audit-readiness.

## 3. Functional and Workflow Requirements

3.1 Describe how your solution supports configurable onboarding workflows for different customer types and risk levels.

3.2 Explain how compliance analysts can review cases, request additional information, record decisions, and maintain an audit trail.

3.3 Describe how the platform supports document upload, validation, retention, and controlled access.

3.4 Explain your approach to notifications, task queues, escalations, and SLA tracking for onboarding cases.

## 4. Integration Requirements

4.1 Describe your API integration approach for identity verification providers, sanctions screening services, core banking systems, CRM, and data warehouses.

4.2 Explain how you handle API authentication, idempotency, retry logic, observability, and reconciliation.

4.3 Describe your approach to event-driven integrations and near-real-time status updates.

## 5. Security, Compliance, and Data Protection

5.1 Describe your data security controls for sensitive identity documents and financial data.

5.2 Explain your privacy and data retention approach for personal data processed during onboarding.

5.3 Describe how your solution supports audit logs, evidence export, traceability, and segregation of duties.

5.4 Provide details of penetration testing, vulnerability management, secure development practices, and third-party risk management.

## 6. Reliability and Operations

6.1 Describe your availability target and operational monitoring approach.

6.2 Explain your backup, restore, disaster recovery, and business continuity capabilities.

6.3 Describe support hours, severity levels, escalation paths, and incident communication.

## 7. Implementation Plan

7.1 Provide your implementation plan for a 16-week MVP followed by phased rollout.

7.2 Describe how you run discovery workshops, requirements validation, integration testing, UAT, and production readiness reviews.

7.3 Explain how your team transfers knowledge to the buyer's product, compliance, and operations teams.

## 8. Commercial and Legal

8.1 Describe your pricing model and any volume-based assumptions.

8.2 Identify contractual assumptions, dependencies on third-party providers, and customer responsibilities.

8.3 State whether your company can accept unlimited liability for data breaches and regulatory penalties.
