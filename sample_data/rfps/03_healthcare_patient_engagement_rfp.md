# RFP: Healthcare Patient Engagement and Appointment Portal

## 1. Overview

A regional healthcare network is seeking a patient engagement portal for appointment booking, visit preparation, secure messaging, post-visit instructions, and integration with electronic health record systems. The solution must protect sensitive health information and be easy to use for patients and care teams.

## 2. Experience and References

2.1 Describe your experience implementing patient-facing portals, healthcare workflows, or other sensitive-data platforms.

2.2 Provide relevant case studies or references from healthcare, public sector, financial services, or other regulated industries.

2.3 Explain how your team works with clinical, operational, IT, privacy, and security stakeholders.

## 3. Patient and Staff Functionality

3.1 Describe how patients can manage appointments, complete pre-visit forms, upload documents, and receive notifications.

3.2 Explain how staff can configure workflows, review patient submissions, and manage exceptions.

3.3 Describe your approach to content management, localization, accessibility, and mobile responsiveness.

## 4. Interoperability and Data Exchange

4.1 Describe your approach to integrating with EHR, scheduling, identity management, notification, and analytics systems.

4.2 Explain whether you support HL7 or FHIR integration patterns.

4.3 Describe how integration failures are monitored, retried, reconciled, and reported.

## 5. Security and Privacy

5.1 Describe your access control model for patients, staff, administrators, and support users.

5.2 Explain how you protect sensitive health data in transit, at rest, and in application logs.

5.3 Describe your approach to consent, privacy notices, data minimization, retention, and data subject requests.

5.4 Explain how audit logs are captured and made available to authorized reviewers.

## 6. Reliability, Support, and Continuity

6.1 Describe your hosting model, availability target, monitoring, alerting, backup, and recovery approach.

6.2 Provide your support model, response times, escalation path, and incident communications process.

6.3 Explain your approach to release management, maintenance windows, and rollback.

## 7. Implementation and Change Management

7.1 Provide your proposed implementation methodology and timeline for pilot and network-wide rollout.

7.2 Describe how you conduct usability testing with patients and staff.

7.3 Explain how you train administrators, support teams, and operational champions.

## 8. Commercial Response

8.1 Provide pricing structure for implementation, licensing, hosting, support, and optional integrations.

8.2 Describe major assumptions, dependencies, and exclusions.
